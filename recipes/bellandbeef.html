Bell Peppers and Beef Recipe

Description

Easy at home recipe with plenty of nutrition

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Bell Peppers
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Sauces
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Put pan on medium to medium-high heat and add oil
  2. Add beef to pan and allow it to gray before removing
  3. Add in veggies and mushrooms and cook until they begin to soften. In the meantime, season as desired with spices.
  4. Add meat back into pan, and add in desired sauces. Continue until sauce is thoroguhly stirred and remove from heat.
